Cryptosystem types

We can distinguish two basic types of cryptosystems:
  • Cryptosystems with symmetric keys
  • Cryptosystems with asymmetric keys

Symmetric keys means that sender and receiver use identical keys, so encryption and decryption can be performed with just one key.
In the second cryptosystem there is one pair of keys which are different. They are closely realted but one is resposible only for encryption and the other for decryption.

There are more differences like way of generation and exchange of keys, encryption and decryption itself but we will explain these aspect in the next sites.
