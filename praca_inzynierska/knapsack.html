Merkle Hellman Cipher

Merkle Hellman cipher is one of the firsts assymetric cryptosystems in the history. It is based on the problem of finding sum of the subset. Both keys public and the private consists of set of the numbers. Moreover private key has two more elements: sum of the set and multiplier coprime with the sum.
