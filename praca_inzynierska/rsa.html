RSA Cypher


RSA cipher - cipher created by Ron Rivest, Adi Szamir and Leonard Adleman. One of the most popular and widely used cipher. It belongs to the asymmetric cryptography system's family with public keys.

RSA algorithm can used for encrypting messages and also for creating digital signatures. The cipher is based on the factorizing huge complex numbers.
