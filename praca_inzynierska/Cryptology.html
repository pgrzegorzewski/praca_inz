Basic information − Cryptology

According to the definition from encyclopaedia − cryptology is the branch of science related with ciphering and safe data transmission. Ciphering is the changing the original text into another one which for people who have no key have no logical sense. Key enables to retrive original text from the encrypted one.

We can divide cryptology into two main parts:
  • Cryptography − connected with securing information by applying cryptography methods

  • Cryptoanalysis − connected with recreating the true information from the ciphers without the key
Cryptology takes its roots from ancient Rome. Since that time cryptology developed remarkably.

Ciphers within the years were chanigng as well as the attacks at them. From trivial shifting alphabet to very complex involving lots of machine computations mathematical transformation which are used nowadays.
The demand for the new, more complex and safer ciphers appeared when known ciphers stopped to be considered as safe ones.
There are two main groups of ciphers considering character transformation:
  • Transposition cipher − encrypted message consists of the same characters as the original message but their order differs

  • Substitution cipher − each character of the original message is exchanged by different one according the predefined rule
