Vigenere cipher

Vigenere cipher belongs to an poliaphabetic, subtitution family of ciphers. To encrypt message we need key as long as the messasge which we would like to encrypt. If we give key which is shorter it is copied as many times as is needed to obtain lenght equall to the message length.

From mathematical point of view encryption process is based on the modular arythmetics. To obtain encrypted letter we are adding two indexes: of sign from the message and the other from the key. Result of the encryption is the modulo from the size of alphabet of this sum.
